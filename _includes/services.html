About Us

We are a group of high school students who love the game of basketball, and look to help kids in our community

Skill Developement

Our coaches have several years of experience in competetive basketball, including at the high school level. We aim to develop our players skills, such as catch-and-shoot jumpers and defensive slides, in order to give them the confidence to use these tools in real games.

Safety

All kids will stay masked, and we will do our part to enforce the county safety rules.

Fun!

We understand that kids have limited opportunities to enjoy outdoor recreations at these times, so we hope to provide a safe space where kids can have fun and let loose!
