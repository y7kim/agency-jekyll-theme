Every year, the New England Fencing Association hosts a fencing competition called the “Pomme de Terre”. On June 19, Team Magical Mongolian Mystery Tour sent a team to compete in the Men’s Foil Team event. The team consisted:</p>

  1. Stephen Jan the adventure ninja himself</li>

  2. Dorian Cohen representing team sponsor “Jaguar Conservation Fund”</li>
  3. Rolando Balboa representing team sponsor “Brooklyn Fencing Academy” </li>
  4. Eddie Gregoriev representing the people of Kazakhstan</li>
    </ol>

    After driving 12 hours crossing 10 state borders running errands on behalf of project Mongol Rally ‘10 that starting at 2AM Friday and included 3 midnight rest stop naps, and a fender bender with a Mercedes, Team MMT arrived in Waltham, Massachusetts at 2AM Saturday morning for a 8AM competition. Sleep deprived and warding off the New England heat and humidity, Team MMT managed to prevail to win the day taking home the first place plaque!</p>
